    • Story About Me

    Thirteen years ago, fresh-faced and eager, I dove headfirst into the corporate scene in the Philippines, right after college. My professional journey took a turn towards the call center industry in 2012, where I managed various US telco accounts.

    This was a period of learning and skill refinement.

    However, after a decade, I felt the pull towards a different path, one that aligned more with my dreams. In 2018, I took a bold step. I bid farewell to the call center life and ventured into the world of freelancing and virtual assistance.

    The journey was far from easy, prompting me to focus on learning about different online freelancing niches. I dived deep into extensive training programs and even enlisted the help of a coach. What truly set me apart was my commitment to continuous learning and growth, allowing me to excel in various roles across different industries.

    Delivering exceptional results with integrity is my top priority. But what does this mean for you? It means you're not just hiring a virtual assistant but a dedicated partner who is committed to your success. Someone who brings a wealth of experience, a passion for learning, and a drive to deliver the best possible results.

    So, let's team up and take your business to new heights!
